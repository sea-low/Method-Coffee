Our process.

Our coffee is created utilizing the very system that the worldâ€™s most sophisticated scientific disciplines use to find answers that are supported by logic and evidence. In the spirit of transparency, we feel it our duty to share an overview of our process.

1. Observation

There are varying degrees of quality offered from different coffee shops.

2. Hypothesis

The perfect cup of coffee can be created.

3. Experimentation

Ethically sourced coffee plants are selected by popular flavor profiles and then cloned until genetic imperfections are eliminated. Their beans are then roasted in our lab and their coffee is distilled.

4. Data Collection

A tasting is held by a diverse group of randomly selected people to try and select the best brews.

5. Analysis

The approved brews are exactly replicated and put into a flavor spectrometer to calculate the essence peaks.

6. Conclusion

Based on the data, our state certified flavor chemists formulate the brew with high quality and locally sourced ingredients. Then, clean organically sourced dihydrogen monoxide is mixed with lab-derived caffeine.
